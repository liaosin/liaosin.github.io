反向传播算法求导的推导过程

Definition:

$L$ : the No. of layers;

$K$ : the No. of neural network’s output;

$S_j$ : the No. of j-th layer’s units;($K=S_L$)

$a_i^{(j)}$ : the output of j-th layer’s i-th unit;

$z_i^{(j)}$ : the input of j-th layer’s i-th unit;

$a^{(j)}$ : the output vector of j-th layer, without bias;

$z^{(j)}$ : the input vector of j-th layer, without bias;

$b^{(j)}$ : the bias of j-th layer;

$\Theta^{(j)}$ : the weight matrix from j-th layer to (j+1)-th layer, dim: $S{j+1}*(S{j}+1)$;

$\Theta_{kn}^{(j)}$ : the weight from j-th layer n-th unit to (j+1)-th layer k-th unit;

$g(x)$ : sigmoid function,
$$
g(x)=\frac{1}{1+e^{-x}}\
$$

$$
\begin{align}
g’(x) & =(-1)\cdot\frac{1}{(1+e^{-x})^2}\cdot e^{-x}\cdot (-1)\
& =\frac{1}{(1+e^{-x})^2}\cdot e^{-x}\
& =\frac{1}{1+e^{-x}}\cdot (1-\frac{1}{1+e^{-x}})\
& = g(x)\cdot (1-g(x))
\end{align}
$$

Neural Network Model:

$$
\begin{cases}
z^{(j+1)}=\Theta^{(j)}*
\begin{bmatrix}
b^{(j)} \
a^{(j)}
\end{bmatrix}\
a^{(j+1)}=g(z^{(j+1)})
\end{cases}
$$

Cost Function:

$$
J(\Theta)=\frac{1}{2}\sum_{k\in K}(a_k^{(L)}-y_k)^2
$$

Diff of Output Layer’s Weight :

$$
\begin{align}
\frac{\partial }{\partial\Theta{kn}^{(L)}} J(\Theta) & =\frac{\partial}{\partial\Theta{kn}^{(L)}} (\frac{1}{2}\sum_{k\in K}(a_k^{(L)}-y_k)^2)\
& =\frac{1}{2}\cdot2\cdot(a_k^{(L)}-y_k)\cdot \frac{\partial ak^{(L)}}{\partial\Theta{kn}^{(L)}}\
& =(a_k^{(L)}-y_k)\cdot \frac{\partial g(zk^{(L)})}{\partial \Theta{kn}^{(L)}}\
& =(a_k^{(L)}-y_k)\cdot \frac{\partial g(z_k^{(L)})}{\partial z_k^{(L)}}
\cdot \frac{\partial zk^{(L)}}{\partial \Theta{kn}^{(L)}}
\end{align}
$$

the second item of left equation:
$$
\begin{align}
\frac{\partial g(z_k^{(L)})}{\partial z_k^{(L)}}
& =g(z_k^{(L)})\cdot (1-g(z_k^{(L)})) \
& =a_k^{(L)}\cdot(1-a_k^{(L)})
\end{align}
$$
the third item of left equation:
$$
\begin{align}
\frac{\partial zk^{(L)}}{\partial \Theta{kn}^{(L)}} & =an^{(L-1)}
\end{align}
$$
total:
$$
\begin{align}
\frac{\partial }{\partial\Theta{kn}^{(L)}} J(\Theta) & =(a_k^{(L)}-y_k)\cdot a_k^{(L)}\cdot(1-a_k^{(L)})\cdot a_n^{(L-1)}\
& = \delta^{(L)} \cdot a_n^{(L-1)}
\end{align}
$$
define: $\delta^{(L)}=(a_k^{(L)}-y_k)\cdot a_k^{(L)}\cdot(1-a_k^{(L)})$

Diff of Hidden Layer’s Weight :

$$
\begin{align}
\frac{\partial }{\partial\Theta{kn}^{(j)}} J(\Theta) & =\frac{\partial}{\partial\Theta{kn}^{(j)}} (\frac{1}{2}\sum_{k\in K}(a_k^{(L)}-yk)^2)\
& =\frac{1}{2}\cdot2\cdot\sum{k\in K}(a_k^{(L)}-y_k)\cdot \frac{\partial ak^{(L)}}{\partial\Theta{kn}^{(j)}}\
& =\sum_{k\in K}(a_k^{(L)}-y_k)\cdot \frac{\partial g(zk^{(L)})}{\partial \Theta{kn}^{(j)}}\
& =\sum_{k\in K}(a_k^{(L)}-y_k)\cdot \frac{\partial g(z_k^{(L)})}{\partial z_k^{(L)}}
\cdot \frac{\partial zk^{(L)}}{\partial \Theta{kn}^{(j)}}
\end{align}
$$

the third item of left equation:
$$
\begin{align}
\frac{\partial zk^{(L)}}{\partial \Theta{kn}^{(j)}} & =\frac{\partial z_k^{(L)}}{\partial a_k^{(j)}}
\cdot \frac{\partial ak^{(j)}}{\partial \Theta{kn}^{(j)}}\
& =\sum_{k\in K}(a_k^{(L)}-y_k)\cdot \frac{\partial g(zk^{(L)})}{\partial \Theta{kn}^{(j)}}\
& =(a_k^{(L)}-y_k)\cdot \frac{\partial g(z_k^{(L)})}{\partial z_k^{(L)}}
\cdot \frac{\partial zk^{(L)}}{\partial \Theta{kn}^{(L)}}
\end{align}
$$
